About

Spring Reception is organized and partially funded by the Computer Science Department annually in April. It is open to students, faculty, staff, family, friends, alumni, and businesses.

Students can reserve spots to showcase their capstone and research projects. They do short presentations and display posters about their work at a networking reception.

The alumni share their insights at the panel discussions.

The event's idea is to allow the students to network with peers and alumni.
